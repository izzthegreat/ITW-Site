Classes

We provide several classes on computers and mobile technology that is geared toward
facilitating communication between laymen and IT.

Basic Tech Jargon for Non-IT Professionals

Is your SQL server down? Do you need to ping your IP? Has it been a awhile since you defragged?
Or worse yet, when your trusty IT asks these questions, do even know what it means?
Come and learn the language of tech.

Virus Protection & Removal

Does your computer freeze up with every third file you download? Do you dread the blue screen
and the spinning wheel? Do you know how to protect yourself? Learn the tips and tricks the
professionals use to remove viruses and protect from infection.

Mobile Literacy

In the 1990's we were amazed to have desktop computer with a GIGABYTE of hard drive space. Now,
the phone in your pocket can blow those old pcs away with ease. But do you know how to harness all
that power? Learn the ins and outs of IOS, Android, and Windows mobile and take full advantage of
the computer in your pocket.

Macintosh for Windows Users

I'll admit it, I made this one out of spite.

For so long, the cult of Apple has convinced us that their world is foreign to us PC goers. Lucky
for you I'm here to translate. Come see the Rosetta stone that translates the mysteries of Mac OSX
into terms even a Windows user can understand.
